How to Use

  • sounds Files: This app uses sounds files from your GitHub directory at sounds/*.wav
  • Hi-hat: Make a quick downward flick motion with your phone
  • Snare: Swing your phone side-to-side like hitting a snare drum
  • Tom: Make a circular motion for the tom-tom drum
  • Calibration: If movements aren't detected, use the calibrate button
  • Mobile: Works best on mobile devices with motion sensors
